Paralegal

Kareena Dalappadu


Joined Exchange Legal Services as a paralegal at the end of January 2023 to gain work experience in a law firm. In the summer 2022, she graduated with an LLB Hons with Human Rights from City University of London and this September she intends to start her studies for the LPC. Prior to completing the LLB, she was in the debate team at school an also a part of the law society. What attracted her most to pursuing a career in law was realising that being a lawyer was about helping, listening and understanding people in order to provide a solution so they can achieve the best outcome in their situations. Kareena has learnt through both her law degree, an at the firm, how the law can be applied differently to each person, as every situation is unique. The part she enjoys most about her work is learning something new. Kareena says she finds it interesting to see the things she has learnt in University being applied to a real case rather than a hypothetical scenario. However, sometimes things she has not come across before can be difficult to understand or apply, but everyone is very welcoming and happy to help. Outside of law, she has also competed in swimming galas for her county and completed grades for both the violin and piano. In her free time she also likes to work on oil paintings and drawings.
