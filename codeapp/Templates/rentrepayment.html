What is Rent Repayment?

A rent repayment order (RRO) is a means for tenants/local authorities to reclaim a maximum of 12 months’ rent paid to their landlord, should they commit an offence. This order must be made within 12 months of the alleged offense. Here at Exchange legal services, if you are a landlord or tenant and you want to make or defend a Rent Repayment Order claim, we will be happy to hear from you. We can lodge a claim on your behalf or defend a claim made against you.

Grounds for RROs

Under UK law, tenants may be entitled to an RRO if their landlord has breached certain legal obligations

Application Process

Tenants can apply for an RRO through the First-tier Tribunal (Property Chamber).

Calculation of RROs

The amount of an RRO will depend on various factors, including the length of the tenancy

Appeal Process

Landlords who disagree with an RRO decision may have the right to appeal to the Upper Tribunal (Lands Chamber)
