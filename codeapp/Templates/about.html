Mission,Vision & Our Story.

Mission

Our mission is to provide outstanding legal services to individuals and businesses, guided by our commitment to excellence, integrity, and client satisfaction. We strive to understand our client's unique needs and objectives and to deliver practical, cost-effective solutions that achieve their desired outcomes. Our goal is to be trusted advisors to our clients, providing them with the highest level of legal expertise, while always putting their interests first.

Vision

Our vision is to be a leading law firm that is recognised for its excellence, innovation, and commitment to its clients and communities. We aim to be a law firm that attracts and retains the best legal talent, fosters a culture of collaboration and learning, and continuously improves its delivery of legal services through the use of technology and innovation. We aspire to be a Law firm that is not only successful in terms of business, but that is also respected and admired for its ethical values, professionalism, and social responsibility

Our Story

Exchange Legal Services started its operation in late 2022. The firm was founded by Mr. Ben Veerapen, a qualified Solicitor of the Supreme Court of England and Wales, and co-partner Hrushi Punjani.

Although a relatively new firm, Ben Veerapen has been in legal practice since December 2002. Since its inception, Exchange Legal Services has hit the floor running with a following and already boasts a large database of clients ranging from the banking industry, overseas enterprises, and small to medium property developers.

We have strong links with the banking industry and enterprises around the world with subsidiaries in the UK. We are an international practice acting for companies based overseas
